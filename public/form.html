SDHA ban appeal form

Greetings!

Upon form submission, your ban appeal request will be reviewed by server moderators. Permanent bans are not handed out lightly. Please compose your case with care.

Most requests will be reviewed within 24 hours of submission (Holidays and weekends not withstanding). Moderator decisions are final.

Upon a successful appeal, a moderator will reach out to you with an invite link for the server. Otherwise, you will receive a message that the ban still stands; it is non-negotiable. Please ensure your DMs are open for at least 24 hours upon submission so we may message you.
